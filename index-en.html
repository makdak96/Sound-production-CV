About me

Born (26.10.1996) and raised in Moscow. My musical career began at the age of 5, when I was sent to the Gnessin Music School. I studied successfully at the music school, so I graduated in two classes at once: piano and drums.

I'm on a sound production course

From the age of 14 I became interested in writing my own electronic music in such programs as "Acid pro" and "Nuendo". At the age of 16, I started DJing, performing in nightclubs, and in 2014, my friends and I organized our first commercial party at the Brooklyn club on Krasnopresnenskaya.

In 2017, with a friend, I organized the MoveTon musical project, where I acted as a music producer (writing arrangements, mixing, mastering), and a friend (Gleb Melentiev) was responsible for the media part, vocals and performances. In 2018, we received our first commercial order, and in 2019, we released our first track on all platforms.

Teaching process for my students

In 2021, the "sound production" direction was opened at the "Codabra" online school, where I was invited as a teacher. I have developed an author's course for 20 lessons for beginner music producers and have taught it to both children and adults.

By 2020, our project has developed, we began to work with such artists as Leonid Agutin, Ani Lorak, Lyubasha. We also received the first state order for the track. From that moment on, orders began to come in on a regular basis, and performances at state events were added to commercial performances.

Soul kitchen party August 2022

In 2019, I was first invited to the Tavrida art cluster, where I met musician and producer Vanya Nanik. In the fall of the same year, I began to help him organize "Soul kitchen" events on a regular basis.

Since I liked the organizational activities, in 2020 I joined the Sav Entertainment company and took part in the Bon Jovi concert in Luzhniki. And already in 2022, as an assistant technical director, I was entrusted with the development and management of the “floating stage” project at the fireworks festival from RosTech.

My goals

After moving to Israel, I understood that a new page in my life was opening and many things would have to start from scratch. Of course, the question of finding a job came to the fore for me, in connection with which I began to get acquainted with local recording studios. In the process of searching, I was surprised to find that almost all popular studios are old-fashioned, and very few of them are oriented towards young people and modern music.

an example of an inexpensive youth studio

Studying the music market in Israel, I realized that there are free niches in the segment of modern youth music. Therefore, the idea of ​​opening a well-equipped and accessible studio for young talents seemed interesting to me. My idea is to create a single creative space where both beginners and experienced musicians can express themselves and work on professional equipment. And also to make it a place of attraction for creative people, where they can communicate, share experiences and feel the atmosphere that helps to create.

an example of an inexpensive youth studio

Since I have experience in creating and teaching an author's course (Development of the course "Sound Production" for the online school "Codabra"), I would like to continue teaching classes within the studio. It is also possible to organize music lectures, seminars and master classes, including free ones.
